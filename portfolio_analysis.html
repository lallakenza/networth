7. Strategic Recommendations

Short Term (0–6 months)

1. Reduce or eliminate JPY margin debt The ¥21.4M JPY short is your most dangerous position. You pay ~€40/month in interest, have a -€1,444 unrealized FX loss, and face asymmetric BOJ risk. Consider closing the Shiseido position (which is up 39% — a strong gain) and repaying the JPY debt. Net savings: ~€500/year in interest + elimination of JPY FX risk.
2. Trim crypto to 15–20% maximum At 29% of NAV, crypto is too large for a portfolio that also uses leverage. Consider trimming ETHA (Ethereum, more volatile, deeper loss) by half. This frees up ~€7K in USD that can reduce margin or diversify.
3. Evaluate Porsche (P911) — down 14.6% Porsche faces structural headwinds (EV transition, China weakness, VW governance). If you don't have high conviction, consider tax-loss harvesting (realize the loss) and rotating into a European auto ETF or broader industrial exposure.

Medium Term (1–3 years)

4. Diversify beyond France 46% France is heavy. Consider adding exposure to: US equities via VWCE/IWDA (global ETFs domiciled in Ireland — 15% WHT vs 30%), Nordic quality stocks, or emerging market ETFs. Irish-domiciled UCITS ETFs are optimal for UAE residents as they pay only 15% US WHT on US dividends.
5. Optimize dividend WHT For French stocks, file Form 5000/5001 to reclaim the excess WHT above the treaty rate (if UAE-France treaty applies). For US exposure, use Ireland-domiciled UCITS ETFs (CSPX, IWDA) instead of US-listed ETFs — this cuts US WHT from 30% to 15%. Replace QQQM/IBIT positions with Irish equivalents when available.
6. Add fixed income / cash buffer With ECB rates at 2.5%, EUR money market funds or short-term bond ETFs yield ~3%. A €30–40K allocation would provide income, reduce volatility, and eliminate the need for margin borrowing. Consider XEON (Xtrackers EUR Overnight Rate ETF).

Long Term (5+ years)

7. Build a core-satellite structure Core (60–70%): Global equity ETF (VWCE or IWDA) + EUR bond allocation. This provides diversification, low fees, and efficient tax treatment. Satellite (30–40%): Individual conviction picks (your French luxury names, crypto, Japan). This structure dramatically reduces concentration risk while maintaining alpha potential.
8. Currency diversification strategy As a UAE resident earning in AED/USD, your deposits are already USD-linked. Your EUR portfolio creates natural currency diversification. Maintain this balance but avoid additional leveraged FX bets (like the JPY carry trade).
9. Target a 1.5–2% dividend yield Your current 0.3% yield means the portfolio generates almost no income. Adding dividend aristocrats or high-quality dividend ETFs (VHYL, SPYD in Irish domicile) would improve cash flow and reduce reliance on capital gains.
