      Analyse d'autofinancement — Cash Flow reel

      Revenus = loyer HC + charges locataire + parking. Charges = tout ce que le proprietaire paye (pret, copro, assurance, PNO, TF). Cash flow = revenus - charges.

      🟢 Garanti (recouvrement certain) 🔴 Incertain (probabilité < 100%)
